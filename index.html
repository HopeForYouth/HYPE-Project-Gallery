About Our Curriculum

The HYPE after-school program is a model of collaboration between HYPE staff, volunteers, teachers, and corporate partners.
We believe that all of these individuals & teams are necessary for a functioning and equitable STE(A)M ecosystem!

Thanks to our team, HYPE students are exposed to:

  • Intro to Programming using Scratch
  • Web Design & Front-End Web Development
  • Game Development
  • Tech Career Exploration
  • Women in Technology Highlights
  • S.M.A.R.T. Goal Setting
  • Resume Writing & Interview Skills
  • Leadership Development
  • Networking and more!

Thank you to all of the partners and volunteers who help us bring tech exposure to HYPE scholars! We can not do this without you!
